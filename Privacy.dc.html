Privacy Policy

Last updated July 5, 2026

Information we collect

When you request an estimate or contact us, we collect the information you provide directly — name, phone number, email address, property address, and details about your roofing needs. We also collect standard technical data (browser type, device, pages visited) when you use our website.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to schedule inspections, prepare estimates, communicate about your project, and — where you've asked for our help — to assist with insurance claims. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Information sharing

We may share information with financing partners you choose to apply through, insurance carriers as part of a claim you've asked us to assist with, and service providers who help us operate our business (such as scheduling and payment processing). Each is bound to use your information only for the purpose we've engaged them for.

Your choices

You may ask us to correct or delete your information at any time by contacting us at hello@oxfordroofing.com. You may opt out of marketing communications while continuing to receive service-related messages about an active project.
