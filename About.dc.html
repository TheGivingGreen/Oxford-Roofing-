About Oxford Roofing

Forty years on St. Louis rooflines.

Our Story

Started on a single ladder in 1985.

Oxford Roofing began as a two-person crew re-shingling homes in south St. Louis. Word-of-mouth work turned into a standing crew, then several — but the standard never changed: one job, done right, with the person who quoted it standing on the roof.

Today we run residential and commercial divisions from the same yard, still owner-operated, still answering our own phones.
